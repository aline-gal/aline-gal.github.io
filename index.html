Data Scientist • Systems & HPC Researcher • Tech for Social Good

  • Open and reproducible research practices

Background

I hold a background in Computer Science and Data Engineering and am currently pursuing graduate research in Systems and Computer Engineering. I have professional experience in large-scale data pipelines, ETL processes, and collaborative research projects with social impact.
